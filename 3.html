VILLAGERS

There are 391 villagers in New Horizons, excluding special characters. All villagers present in New Leaf and most from Welcome amiibo make a return, while 8 new villagers are added: Audie (peppy wolf), Cyd (cranky elephant), Dom (jock sheep), Judy (snooty cub), Megan (normal bear), Raymond (smug cat), Reneigh (sisterly horse), and Sherb (lazy goat).
