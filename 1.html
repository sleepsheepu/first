Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a simulation game for the Nintendo Switch released on March 20, 2020. Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a critical and commercial success, having received positive reviews from critics and selling over 26 million copies as of September 2020, making it the best-selling game in the Animal Crossing series and the second best-selling Nintendo Switch game.
